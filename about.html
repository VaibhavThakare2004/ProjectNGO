About Us

Mission

To create a menstrual hygiene curriculum that teaches menstruation as a vital health milestone, fostering open dialogue and understanding beyond just product access.

Vision

An India where girls live healthy, safe, and educated lives, with menstrual health management recognized as a human right and a platform for sharing stories and health discussions.
