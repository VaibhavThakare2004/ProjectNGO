Anandi Room
Anandi room project is period friendly toilet and room With all the facilities which are required for girls during periods.
Why Anandi Room is required in the School/college?
Due to the lack of adequate water, toilets, places to change pads, disposal of pads
and facilities for rest in case of physical discomfort, girls' drop out rate from schools and colleges is increasing.
Rajotsav
Rajotsav is a celebration of first periods of the girls . We organised this event in community ,school colleges or at jilla parishad Where we felicitate girls who get their first periods in the current year. Event is combination of entertainment and knowledge about periods awareness. We gives gifts and some knowledgeable books to girls. Girls and women enjoy by dancing, singing, skit,rally. Girl feel more comfortable to share and open up about periods.

Poshan
Teaches children the importance of nutrition and physical activity using interactive Webinar and games.
Students work in groups to create charts that show the nutritional needs of different age groups, including babies, adults, and elders. Students research the nutritional content of a vegetable, and sort them by nutritional content.
